ORCA Tech Talks

About the Tech Talks

ORCA hosts monthly Tech Talks featuring speakers working on isolation and compartmentalization across the stack. Our monthly Tech Talks take place on Zoom on the second Thursday of each month at 12-1pm ET. Talks are open to anyone interested in the topic.

Upcoming talks appear on the community calendar. If you would like to give a talk, email vlr9020@nyu.edu.
